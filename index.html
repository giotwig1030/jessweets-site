About Jessweets

Started in 2010, Jessweets Bakery has gradually made its mark in the NYC area, thanks to the two sisters (Jessica and Jeanette) with a vision to provide delicious cakes and sweet treats at an affordable price. The sisters are known as a dynamic team where one compliments the other. Combining Jessica's passion for creating new designs for her fabulous custom cakes and treats and Jeanette's background in Marketing and Design this sister duo is sure to give you the quality and professionalism you seek. Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY with their Dominican parents, they like to fuse their traditional dessert recipes with their knowledge of american desserts; incorporating flavors like, Tropical Tres Leche into their variety of desserts shooters. Focusing on quality and customer satisfaction, these sisters hope to be your go to business for all your dessert needs.
